Our Function


Dipole is an opportunity transferral platform for people who want to participate in seat-limited activities by exchanging tickets with existing ticket-owners online. One specific use case for Dipole.io would be for educational opportunities and activities, such as course registration, meal plans, and research/testing groups. As a student, you can request and search for postings that give you a chance to join classes that are already full by swapping your current classes with your peers. As an organizer, you may use Dipole to post opportunities and groups, and let our system provide flexibility in how your members are organized and assigned to those groups. As the platform matures, we will extend Dipole to support increasingly general opportunities such as volunteering, with the end goal of increasing the flexibility for opportunity reassignment to maximize win-win situations.
